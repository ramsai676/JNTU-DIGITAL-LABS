ELCS Laboratory

Resources for improving English Language and Communication Skills.

  • Exercise 1: Listening & Phonetics

    Practice with speech sounds, minimal pairs, and consonant clusters.

  • Exercise 2: Word Stress & Intonation

    Learn basic rules of word accent, stress shift, and sentence intonation.

  • Exercise 3: Pronunciation Practice

    Practice neutralizing mother tongue influence (MTI) and learn British vs. American pronunciation.

  • Exercise 4: Listening Comprehension

    Practice listening for general and specific details with comprehension tests.

  • Exercise 5: Public Speaking & Group Discussion

    Learn strategies for JAM sessions, making presentations, and effective group discussions.
